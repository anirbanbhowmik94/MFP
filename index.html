About Us
MOVING FRAME PRODUCTIONS (MFP) is a professional Production House, Film Institute & Digital Entertainment Channel in New Delhi, India. It was founded in the year 2012 by Mr. Rohit Kumar Trivedi. We are crackerjack in Short Films, Promotional Videos, Corporate Videos, Education & Training Videos, Ad Films, Web Series, Event & Wedding Films, Feature Films, Fashion & Product Photo Shoot, Explainers (Business Explainers & Product Demo), Music Videos (HD, Full HD, 4K), etc. We strive to provide an exceptional service that always delivers on time & in your budget.
